Introducing Private Scratch Wallet for the iExec WTF Hackathon.

Scratch Wallet limits downside. Private Scratch Wallet keeps the downside policy private.

Autonomous DeFi agents should not expose bankroll limits, stop-loss thresholds, risk modes, or execution rules. This dApp uses a Nox-style confidential policy flow and publishes only commitments and decisions to Ethereum Sepolia.
