Introducing **Private Scratch Wallet** for the iExec WTF Hackathon.

Scratch Wallet limits downside. Private Scratch Wallet keeps the downside policy private.

Autonomous DeFi agents should not reveal sensitive risk rules like bankroll, max trade size, stop-loss, risk mode, and execution thresholds. Private Scratch Wallet uses a Nox-style confidential policy flow and a Sepolia registry so users can commit to auditable decisions without exposing the full policy.
